v0.1.0 — voice guardrails for coding agents
Stays quiet. Speaks up when it matters.
A supervision daemon that watches your coding agent, narrates dangerous operations, and lets you slap your MacBook to interrupt.
[narration]
Speaks out loud when the agent attempts dangerous or irreversible ops.
[drift]
Catches when the agent wanders from your original goal mid-session.
[recap]
Summarises what happened and how much the session cost when done.
[slap]
Physically slap your Mac to pause the agent. Apple Silicon only.
[privacy]
Cloud, hybrid, or fully local — nothing leaves your machine in local mode.
[silent]
Only speaks on high-signal events. No noise while things are going well.
$ brew install AMOORCHING/pillow/pillow-cli
$ pillow setup
$ bash plugin/install.sh
